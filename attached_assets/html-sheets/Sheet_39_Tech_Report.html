4.00Specification Proposed : :-The following specifications are proposed in this estimete as under -
The administrative sanction for Construction of High Level Bridge having 8 Nos. span of 10.00 M each and open foundation has been proposed. The substructure in RCC wall type piers in M-25 and RCC solid slab simply supprted dacking also in M-30 (Design Mix Concreate). The solid slab has deck slab proposed to be properly Anchored into The pier cap against uplifting and drag. The pier footing has also proposed to be similarly anchored into the rocky strata.
(a) Foundation Concreate P.C.C. M-15 grade 20 mm nominal size aggregate.
(b) Foundation Footing of Piers - it has been proposed of RCC mix M-25. The annular space filled with M-15 with 20 mm nominal Size stone aggregate upto the top of the rock.
(C) Foundation Footing of Abutments - It has been proposed of RCC mix M-20 The annular space filled with P.C.C. M-15 grade concreate with 20mm nominal Size stone aggregate upto the top of the rock.
(D) Piers, Pier caps - These has been proposed of RCC Mix M-25
(E) Abutments, Abutment caps and Dirt wall - These has been proposed of RCC Mix M-25
(F) Deck Slab - The simply supported deck slab has been proposed of R.C.C. mix M-30. The deck slab shall be anchored at both ends with 25mm dia anchor bolts to prevent it from uplift and the drag of flowing water.
(G) Guard Stone - RCC guardd stones has been proposed of mix M-25 as per recommendations of IRC special publiciation on Submersible bridges. Ref IRC:SP:82-2008
(H) Wearing Coat - M-30 concreate with reinforcement as per IRC recommendation is proposed. The average thickness of the wearing coat 75mm.
(I) Approach slab - Approach slab has been proposed of RCC M-25 overlaid The wearing coat similar to that on deck slab has been proposed provided over the approach slab.
(J) Filling behind Abutment & Wing walls- The back Fill has been proposed of locally available granular material it shall be compacted as per specifications.
(K) Earth work in cutting for grade improvement on approches has been proposed.
