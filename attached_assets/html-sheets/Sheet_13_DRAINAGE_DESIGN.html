DRAINAGE CALCULATION

Flow Rate (Q) = 0.25 × A × i
Where: A = catchment area, i = slope intensity
For 2.0% slope and 15m × 7.5m area:
Q = 0.25 × 112.5 × 0.02 = 0.56 m³/s

Required Pipe Capacity:
Using 150mm pipe with 2% slope: 0.025 m³/s (Inadequate)
Solution: 2× 150mm pipes or 1× 200mm pipe
